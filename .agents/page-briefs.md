**One job:** Get a business owner to book a free strategy call

see this is professional, affordable, and different from generic agencies.

1. Who this is for (small businesses who want to grow)
2. What makes this different (AI-powered, fast, affordable, Canva-first process)

1. Hero — headline, subheadline, CTA
2. Marquee — key selling points
3. Services overview — 3 packages, brief descriptions, pricing
4. Portfolio preview — diverse case study cards across industries
5. About — founder's background, credentials, process
6. Process — 4 steps
7. FAQ (schema-eligible)

9. Footer

**Visitor state:** Already interested. Evaluating cost vs value.
Main objections: too expensive? What do I get? Will it work?

**Section flow:**
1. Brief intro — one paragraph establishing broad capability
2. Package cards — Presence / Visibility / Growth
3. What every package includes
4. Process overview
5. FAQ — pricing, timeline, what they need to provide
