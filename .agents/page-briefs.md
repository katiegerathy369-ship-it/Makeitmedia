# Make It Media — Page Briefs

## Homepage /

**Primary keyword:** naturopath website design australia
**One job:** Get a qualified health practitioner to book a free strategy call

**Visitor state:** Aware they need a better website. Sceptical. Need to immediately
see this is different from generic agencies.

**Above the fold must show:**
1. Who this is for (health practitioners specifically)
2. What makes this different (founder is a naturopath)
3. What the primary action is (book a call)

**Section flow:**
1. Hero — headline, subheadline, CTA
2. Credibility — founder's health background, quick proof
3. The problem — why generic agencies fail health practitioners
4. Services overview — 3 packages, brief descriptions, pricing
5. Portfolio preview — 2–3 case study cards
6. Testimonials
7. FAQ (schema-eligible)
8. Final CTA

**Never on this page:**
- Generic "we build beautiful websites" copy
- Tech stack details in hero
- Pricing as the first thing seen

## Services /services

**Primary keyword:** website design for health practitioners
**One job:** Help visitor choose the right package and move toward booking

**Visitor state:** Already interested. Evaluating cost vs value.
Main objections: too expensive? What do I get? Will it work?

**Section flow:**
1. Brief intro — one paragraph reaffirming the niche
2. Package cards — Presence / Visibility / Growth
3. What every package includes
4. Process overview
5. FAQ — pricing, timeline, what they need to provide
6. CTA

**Copy rules:**
- Lead every package with the outcome, not the feature list
- Mark Visibility as most popular
- Be specific: "5 pages" not "multi-page website"
- Don't hide pricing — transparency is a differentiator

**One job:** Build confidence through proof

## About /about

**Primary keyword:** make it media naturopath web design
**One job:** Build trust through founder's story + credentials

**Section flow:**
1. Opening in third person (LLM-extractable): "Make It Media is an Australian web design agency..."
2. Founder story — health science background, why this niche
3. How this differs from generic agencies
4. Credentials
5. CTA

- Lead with credential before story
- Specific facts over vague passion statements

## Contact /contact

**Primary keyword:** book naturopath website design consultation
**One job:** Convert interest into a booked call — remove ALL friction

**Must include:**
- What to expect from the call (3 bullet points)
