# Make It Media — Product Marketing Context

## The Business

**Agency:** Make It Media
**Founded by:** Kate (Bachelor of Health Science, Naturopathy + Graphic Design background)
**Type:** AI-powered web design and digital marketing agency
**Primary market:** Australian small businesses across all industries
**Secondary niche:** Health practitioners (dedicated landing page at /health-practitioners)
**Positioning:** "Small business owner turned AI developer" — credibility-led, not generic agency. Kate understands what it's like to run a business and need marketing that actually works.
**Brand theme:** Growth — the plant logo represents small businesses growing with the right foundation.

## Target Client — Primary (Homepage)

**Primary ICP:** Australian small business owners who need a professional website and marketing

**Demographics:**
- Small business owners, solo operators, or small teams (1–10 people)
- Predominantly 25–50
- Based in Australia
- Revenue $50k–$500k+
- Industries: health & wellness, professional services, hospitality, trades, coaching, creative, retail

**Voice of customer — verbatim phrases:**
- "I don't have time to figure out websites"
- "I want something professional that doesn't look like a template"
- "I need more clients but I hate marketing myself"
- "I want to look established even though I'm just starting out"
- "My website is embarrassing but I don't know where to start"
- "I just want it to work and bring in leads"
- "I want my website to do the work for me"

**Biggest fears:**
- Wasting money on something that doesn't work
- Looking unprofessional or like a template
- The process being complicated and time-consuming
- Not getting ROI
- Being locked into something they can't update

## Target Client — Secondary (Health Practitioners Page)
